Tteokbokki

  • 1 pound of cylinder shaped rice cake (tteok), bought or homemade

  • 4 cups of water

  • 7 dried acnhovies

  • 1/3 cup of hot pepper paste (gochujang)

  • 1 table spoon of Korean hot pepper flakes (gochugaru)

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 3 green onions

  • 1/2 pound fishcake (optional)

  • 2pc boiled egg (optional)

    		•

    Boil anchovies over medium high heat for 15 minutes (don't cover)

    Combine gochujang, gochugaru, and sugar in a bowl.

    Remove anchovies in pot and add rice cake

    Add the mixture, green onion, and fishcake

    Stir gently until the sauce thickens

    Serve and Enjoy!
    Kimchi Jiggae

  • 1 pound kimchi (cut into bite size)

  • 1/4 cup kimchi brine

  • 1/2 pount pork belly

  • 1/2 package of tofu (optional)

  • Green onions

  • 1 medium onion

  • 1 teaspoon salt and sugar

  • 2 teaspoon gochugaru

  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil

  • 2 ups of anchovy stock (chicken or beef broth)

    		•

    Place kimchi and kimchi brine in a pot. Add pork and onion.

    Slice 2 green onions and add to the pot

    Add salt, sugar, and hot pepper paste

    Cook for 10 minutes in medium high heat

    Mix in the seasonings with spoon. Add the tofu on the top.

    Cover and cook for another 10-15 minutes

    Chop green onion and put it on top of the stew.

    Serve and Enjoy!

    Bibimbap

  • Beef

  • Vegetable (Carrots, mushroom, spinach)

  • Cucumber

  • White onion

  • Egg

  • Gochujang

  • garlic

  • sesame oil

  • Rice vinegar

  • Soy Sauce

  • Honey

    		•

    Mix garlic, sesame oil, honey, rice vinegar, and soy sauce

    Pour the mixture in the beef and let it marinate for 30 minutes

    Heat up a pan (skillet) over high heat

    Cook the marinated beef

    Place the carrot and cucumber into a separate bowls and toss 1/4 salt onto it

    Heat up pan and cook mushrooms for 2 minutes then remove

    Cook cucumber and carrots for 4 minutes

    Saute spinach, add 1/2 minced garlic and salt, and drain excess liquid

    Fry eggs in a pan

    Plate all of the food in one bowl

    Serve and Enjoy!

    Bingsu

  • 4 1/2 cups milk

  • 1/2 cup condensed milk

  • Toppings (e.g. fruits, bean, condensed mill, cocoa powder, etc.)

    		•

    Mix thr condensed milk and milk using a whisk

    Pour it into a freezer container

    Cover the container and freeze it overnight

    If it has frozen, remove from the container and wrap it in a plastic wrap

    Use a grater or food processor to shave the milk block

    Add your preferred toppings on top

    Serve and Enjoy!
    Dalgona

  • White sugar (can be brown)

  • Baking Soda

  • Metal ladle

    		•

    Heat up your stove

    Put the ladle on top of the stove (with open fire)

    Let it heat

    Melt the 2 spoonful of sugar on the ladle

    Add a pinch of baking soda

    Mix gently

    Pour it on a container with parchment paper

    Let it cool down for 5 minutes

    Serve and Enjoy!
