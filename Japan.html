Food Recipe Instructions
Tempura

  • shrimp or prawn

  • all purpose flour

  • cornstarch

  • egg

  • water (cold or with ice)

    		•

    1. Prepare the Shrimp. Take out the belly (black thing) and shell from the shrimp.

    2. Toss it in a bowl with cornstarch

    3. Mix cold egg with water in a bowl.

    4. Dip shrimp to the egg after coating it with cornstarch.

    5. Preheat your pan and heat up oil.

    6. Cook shrimp until light-golden brown.

    7. Serve and Enjoy!
    Ramen

  • Ramen noodles

  • Garlic and ginger

  • Broth (chicken or beef) depends on you

  • vegetables

  • cheese, eggs, etc. (optional)

    		•

    1. Prepare a boiling water.

    2. Make your own broth

    3. Put in your noodles

    4. Add the vegetables

    5. Serve and Enjoy!
    Sushi

  • Rice

  • seasoning

  • Nori

  • Fruit (mango, cucumber, etc.)

  • Filling (crabstick, pork, shrimp, etc.)

    		•

    1. Prepare the rice.

    2. Add seasoning to your rice

    3. Flatten the rice and add the filling

    4. Add nori to cover the rice

    5. Cut into smaller pieces.

    6. Serve and Enjoy!
    Anmitsu

  • Kanten Jelly or agar-agar

  • 2-3 cups of water

  • 2 teaspoon sugar

  • Lemon juice (optional)

  • Fruit (mango, peaches, orange, kiwi, etc.)

    		•

    1. Soak agar-agar in water until it soften

    2. After, tear into pieces

    3. Put it into a boiling water

    4. Make syrup using lemon and sugar

    5. Serve and Enjoy!
    Japanese Cheesecake

  • egg whites

  • 1/4 lemon juice (depends on you)

  • 1/4 granulated sugar

  • Cream cheese

  • Milk

  • Butter

  • Cornstarch and flour

    • Fruits (Any, optinal).

    1. Separate the egg whites from yolks

    2. Melt butter, cream cheese, and 1/4 sugar into low heat.

    3. Remove pot from heat, add the yolks and mix gently.

    4. Pre-heat the oven

    5. Add batter and beaten eggs

    6. Bake for 30 minutes

    7. Serve and Enjoy!
