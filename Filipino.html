Adobo

  • Chicken or pork

  • Soy Sauce

  • Vinegar (optional)

  • Sugar

  • Salt and Pepper (whole not crushed)

  • Laurel leaves or bay leaves

  • Garlic

  • Water

    		•

    Minced the garlic and mix it with chicken

    Marinate the chicken using soy sauce

    Heat the pot and put the marinated chicken

    Add more soy sauce (optional)

    Add vinegar and sugar(optional)

    Add pepper

    Add water

    Add laurel leaves or bay leaves

    Serve and Enjoy!

    Sisig

  • 1/4 Pork belly

  • Knorr liquid seasoning

  • 2pc onion

  • Chicken/pork liver (can be in canned)

  • Ground black pepper

  • butter

  • Chicharon (crushed)

  • Cooking oil

  • Egg

    		•

    Boil the pork belly in the pot

    After, minced the pork belly

    Heat pan, add oil, and Saute onion

    Add chicken/pork liver

    Add minced meat and crushed chicharon

    Add black pepper and knorr seasoning

    Add mayonnaise

    Put in a sizzling plate with butter and egg on top

    Serve with calamansi and Enjoy!

    Tapsilog

  • Beef Tapa

  • Egg

  • Rice

  • Soy Sauce

  • Salt and Sugar

    		•

    Heat up pan with cooking oil

    Add beef tapa

    Add soy sauce and sugar

    Remove the beef from the pan

    Fry egg and remove it from the pan

    Mix rice with garlic and soysauce

    Serve and Enjoy!
    Leche Flan

  • Egg

  • Condensed Milk

  • Vanilla extract

  • Brown Sugar

  • llanera (container)

    		•

    Separate the white eggs from yolk

    Beat the egg yolks

    Add condensed milk and vanilla to the yolk

    Pre-heat a pot

    Put the llanera on top of the stove

    Add the brown sugar on the llanera and spread it evenly

    Add the yolk mixture on the llanera

    Cover the llanera using aluminum foil

    Steam and wait for 35 minutes

    After, let it cool down before putting in the refrigerator

    Serve and Enjoy!
    Turon

  • Spring roll wrapper

  • Banana (Saging na Saba)

  • Brown Sugar

  • Jackfruit or langka (optional)

  • egg

    		•  Get a spring roll wrapper

    Place the banana in the middle

    Add Jackfruit next to the banana

    Add 1 teaspoon of sugar

    Fold the wrapper and seal using egg

    Roll it with brown sugar

    Deep fry

    Serve and Enjoy!
